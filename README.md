## About

This module introduces the foundational mindset and mechanics of working with GenAI in complex, real-world data workflows. Students learn to decompose ambiguous goals into AI-compatible tasks, plan multi-step prompting workflows, and test those workflows to see where GenAI excels—and where human oversight is essential.

By the end, learners will be able to design, execute, and refine AI-augmented solutions with a clear sense of the roles, risks, and rewards of collaborating with large language models.

## Content

| Lesson | Est. Delivery Time | Skills |
| ------ |:------------------:| ------ |
| [Decomposing and planning an augmented workflow](./microlesson-01/README.md) | ~20 min | Break a complex goal into GenAI-compatible subtasks and design a clear, multi-step prompt workflow. |
| [Testing and troubleshooting your GenAI workflow](./microlesson-02/README.md) | ~20 min | Execute your workflow, identify breakdowns, and analyze how LLM behavior affects workflow success. |
| [Reflection and knowledge check](./microlesson-03/README.md) | ~20 min | Reflect on GenAI strengths and limits, and synthesize key takeaways for real-world use. |
| **Total content**                                   | 60 min | Design, execute, and evaluate GenAI workflows in real-world data scenarios. |

### Prerequisites

- Basic familiarity with GenAI interfaces like ChatGPT
- Understanding of your own domain’s common data tasks
- Comfort with collaborative problem-solving

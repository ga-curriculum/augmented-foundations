## Augmented Foundations: Reflection and Knowledge Check

Let’s pause and reflect on what you discovered about designing workflows for GenAI.

---

## Group reflection

Discuss as a group or jot down answers:

- Name one task in your workflow that GenAI did *really well*.
- Name one that needed *human intervention*.
- Why do you think GenAI succeeded or struggled with those steps?

---

> 📚 **Remember:**  
LLMs are great at pattern recognition, summarization, and language transformation.  
They struggle with ambiguous requests, missing context, or tasks needing judgment or privacy.

---

## Knowledge check

- How would you improve your workflow next time?
- Where in your real-world work could you apply this approach?
- When would you *not* want to rely on GenAI?

---

## Key takeaway

Designing effective GenAI workflows is all about **clarity, structure, and iteration**.  
You get the best results when you treat AI as an *augmenter*, not a replacement.
